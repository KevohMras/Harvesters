Safaricom Enterprise Business Unit (EBU)

Safaricom's Enterprise Business Unit (EBU) is the business-to-business arm of the company that provides innovative and reliable business solutions to organizations of all sizes. Whether you're a small business, large enterprise, non-governmental organization (NGO), or public sector organization, EBU has tailored solutions that meet your needs and help drive your business forward.

EBU's Comprehensive Service Offerings

EBU provides a range of services designed to empower businesses to be more efficient,
competitive, and innovative. Below is an overview of the key services offered:

Broadband Connectivity

EBU provides high-speed internet connectivity over LTE, tailored for businesses requiring stable and scalable internet access. Advanced features such as port forwarding, traffic shaping, and bandwidth burstability ensure businesses can optimize their network traffic based on demand. This is achieved by use of routers and other network connectivity devices.

Technology Solutions

EBU's technology solutions support the digital transformation of businesses, including services for:

  • Fleet Management: Efficient tracking and management of vehicles for cost reduction.
  • Information Storage: Secure cloud storage for essential data.
  • Disaster Recovery: Ensure minimal downtime with effective recovery strategies.
  • Remote Tracking: Monitor business assets in real-time for better control.

Lipa Na M-PESA Merchant Service

The Lipa Na M-PESA service enables enterprise customers to receive payments for goods and services via M-PESA, providing businesses with an easy and secure way to accept payments without the need for physical POS systems. These include :

  • Pochi la Biashara
  • Buy Goods
  • Paybill
